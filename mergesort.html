Mergesort - a divide and Conquer algorithm. Divides input array in two halves, calls itself for two halves and then merges the two sorted halves.
MergeSort(arr[], l, r) If r > l: 1. Find the middle point to divide the array in two halves m = (l+r)/2; 2. Call mergeSort for first half: mergeSort(arr, l, m); 3. Call mergeSort for second half: mergeSort(arr, m+1, r); 4. Merge the two halves: call merge(arr, l, m, r);
